## About

Michele Ghezzi is an agronomist and technical consultant working with agricultural businesses in berries, horticulture, nurseries, green areas, and multiple field and tree crops.

His work focuses on sustainable technical planning, biological fertilization, organic matter quality, beneficial microorganisms, and agronomic decisions grounded in site, soil, and water conditions.
